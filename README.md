# **Nykaa E-Commerce Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

## **Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing product data from Nykaa to uncover trends in pricing, discounts, ratings, reviews, and brand performance. The dataset was collected through Python-based web scraping and processed using standard data-cleaning techniques. The goal is to understand customer behavior patterns and generate insights that support decision-making for e-commerce scenarios.

---

## **Objectives**

* Collect product data from Nykaa through automated scraping.
* Clean, format, and structure raw data for analysis.
* Explore pricing, discounts, ratings, and review patterns.
* Identify top-performing categories and brands.
* Visualize trends to support e-commerce insights.

---

## **Tech Stack**

* **Python**
* **Pandas**
* **NumPy**
* **Matplotlib / Seaborn**
* **BeautifulSoup / Selenium**
* **Jupyter Notebook**

---

## **Key Insights**

* Variation in pricing and discount strategies across categories.
* Relationship between discount percentage and product visibility.
* Rating and review patterns that influence customer engagement.
* Identification of high-performing brands and product segments.

---

## **Workflow**

1. **Scraping:** Product details captured from Nykaa using Selenium + BeautifulSoup.
2. **Cleaning:** Removing duplicates, handling missing values, formatting columns.
3. **EDA:** Statistical summaries, correlation checks, trend analysis.
4. **Visualization:** Category-wise comparisons, discount vs price plots, rating distributions.
5. **Insights:** Actionable findings for marketing, pricing, and product strategy.

